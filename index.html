I am a research scientist in Machine Learning at DeepMind.

I received a Diploma di Laurea in Mathematics from University of Bologna and a PhD in Machine Learning from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (IDIAP Research Institute).

I worked in several Machine Learning and Statistics research groups: the Empirical Inference Department at the Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, the Machine Intelligence and Perception Group at Microsoft Research Cambridge, and the Statistical Laboratory, University of Cambridge.

My research interests are based around Bayesian & causal reasoning, graphical models, variational inference, time-series models, deep learning, and fairness.

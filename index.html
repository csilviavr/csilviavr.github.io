I am a research scientist in Machine Learning at DeepMind. I received a Diploma di Laurea in Mathematics from University of Bologna and a PhD in Machine Learning from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (IDIAP Research Institute). I worked as a post-doctoral researcher at the Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics in the Department of Empirical Inference led by Bernhard Schölkopf, and at Microsoft Research Cambridge in the Machine Learning and Perception Group; and I held a Marie-Curie Intra-European Fellowship at the Statistical Laboratory University of Cambridge.

My research interests are based around Bayesian reasoning, graphical models, approximate inference, time-series models, deep learning, and reinforcement learning.
